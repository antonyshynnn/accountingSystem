University's history

Lviv Polytechnic National University is the oldest technical higher educational institution in Ukraine and East Europe

It was founded in 1816. University consists of 17 institutes, more than 100 departments; autonomous, additional and general subdivisions. For a long time it was believed that the origin of Lviv Polytechnic National University dates back to 1844, when the Austrian government formed the Technical Academy in Lviv.
