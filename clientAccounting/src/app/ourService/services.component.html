Our Departments

The training of highly qualified specialists for the current and future needs of society is provided by three graduating departments:

Software Development

Acquisition of knowledge, skills and practical skills related to the analysis and formulation of requirements, creation, maintenance and quality assurance of software..

Computer Science Department

Development and research of smart enterprise systems, smart home, smart city, smart grids, etc. Use of methods of computational intelligence in mobile robotic systems and augmented reality systems.

Information Systems and Networks

Acquisition of theoretical and practical knowledge, skills and understanding of information systems and technologies (ICT), which contribute to the social stability and mobility of graduates in the labor market.
